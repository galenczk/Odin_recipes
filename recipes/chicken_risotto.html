Odin's Chicken Risotto

Description

This is a delicious and easy to make dish that pleases even the most mighty gods. Made in a single baking sheet, this dish combines meat, rice, and vegetables for a healthy all-around meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Green Onion
  • Chicken Stock
  • White Wine
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook down the vegetables in a large pot. Add the white wine and allow to reduce.
  2. Place rice in a 9x13 baking dish and cover with vegetables.
  3. Pour chicken stock over the veggies and rice.
  4. Place chicken on top of all this and cover dish with foil.
  5. Bake for one hour, take off foil and bake for another 30 mins. Finish with broiler for 2-3 minutes if chicken is desired more crispy.
